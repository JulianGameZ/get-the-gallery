GENRES

Neutral Nouveau: This person has a monochromatic style, and admires the versatility of neutral tones. The neutral nouveau shopper acknowledges the timeless sophistication of a neutral color palette, but also demands modern iterations of neutrality within their home interiors.

 

Modern Pop: Individuals who are not afraid of color, and who actively seek out big and bold trends. The Modern Pop persona is a one in which craves color and statement pieces. (possibly individuals who would like graffiti pieces)

 

Urban Dweller: This genre is for individuals with an eclectic style. The urban dweller admires by both vintage and modern trends. This individual admires the sleek and modern aspects of city life, and can include photography and graffiti pieces.

 

Classic Edge: This genre is for individuals who like to push boundaries, and admire tougher textiles. This person is interested in provocative art pieces that make a statement. (We can suggest a variety of art pieces to this person, including photography, graffiti pieces, and nude pieces)
